Hello, I’m Falcon, an embedded hardware & software guy. I am passionate about electronic stuff since I was 10. I also like mountain biking and long distance trail running, helps me be more disciplined.

Before I went to study a Higher Diploma in Electronics and Information Engineering, I was craving about electronic theories, assembly language and PCB fabrication. I would go to borrow book from library. Overnight programming and soldering… (maybe just a LED marquee circuit). I couldn’t be happier at that time with microprocessors.

Many years after, I garduated from post-graduate school. These 15+ years with my whole heart in this field, I never felt enough on electronics.

From freelance projects, electrical home appliances to IoT things in my path. I am still very looking forward to contributing the beautiful world of electronics consumer products.
